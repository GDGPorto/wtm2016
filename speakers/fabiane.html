Fabiane Valente

Fabiane is presently an invited teaching assistant of Information Systems at FEP. Previously, she has worked for ten years as Manager in a Big4 company. Fabiane is also a Ph.D. candidate in Management, with research interests in AI modeling for marketing and business strategies. She is currently championing CoderDojo Feira, a local-based initiative that is part of a worldwide movement of free, volunteer-led, community-based programming clubs for young people.
