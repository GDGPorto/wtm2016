Susana Boavida

Susana Boavida has a degree in Computer Science and she has focused her career on the software quality area, having worked in challenging sectors like space and aeronautics. Currently, she works as a Quality Engineer at WIT Software and a part of her time is dedicated to teaching children from 9 to 11 years old to code. As a women with a 15 year path in the (predominantly male) IT sector, she is particularly eager to inspire girls to explore Computer Science and Engineering and to show them that anyone can code.
