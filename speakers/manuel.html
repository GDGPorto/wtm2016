Manuel Ferreira

Manuel Fernando is both passionate about life and technology, and so he graduated first in Biology, and then in Computer Engineering. He devoted his career to teaching. In his spare time, he mentored a solar energy club, where children of all ages created models of solar cars and boats (and even a small plane) to compete in a national circuit.

He is currently working on a project to teach children how to code, focusing in creating a new generation of problem-solvers.
