Workshop: Programação para crianças

Technology is a fundamental part of today’s world. Understanding it is a basic knowledge that every child should have access to. It’s vital that students in every school should have the opportunity to learn computer science, just like biology, chemistry or algebra.

In this workshop, we are going to use Code.org a non-profit dedicated to expanding access to computer science, and increasing participation by women and underrepresented minorities.

We are going to do an Hour of Code where the basic concepts of Computer Science with drag and drop programming will be addressed. This is a game-like, self-directed tutorial starring video lectures by Bill Gates, Mark Zuckerberg, Angry Birds and Plants vs. Zombies. Learn repeat-loops, conditionals, and basic algorithms.
